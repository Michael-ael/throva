Throva Yield Units
The Smarter way to reward wealth behavior.

Throva Yield Units or TYUs are digital yield credits earned when you help grow the Throva Engine. They’re not just referral points. They’re redeemable, convertible value units backed by Throva capital and yours to cash in or compound.

Sign Up

What are TYUs worth?

TYUs are liquid and flexible.

  • Convert 1000 TYUs = $500
    off capital deployment fees.
  • 10,000 TYUs = $5,000
    redeemable into your Throva
    account.
  • 20,000 TYUs = $11,000
    (increased value if held
    over 6 months).
  • 25,000 TYUs = Entry to Throva Sovereign Circle & 1 on 1 portfolio
    build with desk.

You can choose to:

  • Redeem them as real capital credits.

  • Use them to unlock private deal tiers or retreats.

  • Gift them to family (yes, they're transferable once verified).



The tech behind it

TYUs aren’t just “points.” They’re token ready assets on our internal ledger, backed by:

  • Deflationary design (limited issuance, 50,000 TYUs max per quarter).

  • Capital margin from Throva’s performance pool.

  • AI monitored allocation (no bots or gaming only real verified activity).
